ANNAPURNA BASE CAMP

Annapurna Base Camp Trek probably is one of the most popular trekking itineraries in Nepal. Famous for its diverse scenery, excellent mountain views, and rich cultural interaction opportunities, ABC trek is a beautiful experience. On this journey, trekkers go past terraced rice paddies, quiet mountain villages, verdant pine, rhododendron, and bamboo forest. Moreover, it is a splendid chance to see the glacial amphitheater of the mightiest peaks in the Himalayas situated near Annapurna Sanctuary.

You will be literally standing in the shadow of world-famous mountains such as Dhaulagiri, Annapurna South, Machapuchare, and Annapurna I, the world’s tenth highest peak. Your itinerary also takes you to Poon Hill, an iconic vantage point. From Poon Hill, one gets to see these peaks’ ethereal beauty shining golden due to the magnificent sunrise. The ABC trek further takes you to Machapuchare Base Camp, where fantastic views of Hiunchuli and Gangapurna await your arrival.

In addition to the spectacular views, the Annapurna Base Camp Trek allows visitors to experience an authentic rural Nepali lifestyle. The trekking trail goes through villages settled on the foothills of the Annapurna range. The trekking course is home to ethnic Magar and Gurung villages. These communities follow ancient mountain cultures that have a heavy influence on Buddhism and Hinduism. Apart from that, they also follow animism that views the existence of spirit in nature’s component. The temples, monasteries, Gompas adorn the trekking trail. These religious shrines represent the solid religious heritage of the locale. As you hike through the course, you will get to connect with locals and their pastoral lifestyle. Everyone you meet will captivate you with their unwavering warmth and hospitality. Similarly, teahouses ran by the locals will cater to your comfort while you’re on the trail.

Furthermore, the enduring popularity of the Annapurna Base Camp Trek is due to its accessibility. Pokhara, the trek’s start point, is accessible by flight and scenic drive on the highway. Pokhara is a beautiful lakeside town with good mountain viewpoints and a variety of shopping and dining options. As a result, it is an excellent destination for vacation in its own right. Hikers who want to combine their mountain trek with some city exploration will find this itinerary particularly appealing. Consequently, you will find several exciting trekking routes once you are on the trail. Thus, trekkers have few options to customize their trek itinerary as per their desire. Additionally, the trek is moderately complex and is doable even without any prior trekking experience.
