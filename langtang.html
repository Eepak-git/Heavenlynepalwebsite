LANGTANG

Just 30 km away in the north of Kathmandu valley is the enriching trekking destination of Nepal. With more than 70 glaciers embellishing the mighty Himalayan peaks of Langtang Himal, Langtang valley is simply a fantastic place to be. Langtang valley trek adventure begins from Syabrubensi via the rural villages of Langtang valley. The Langtang district is the third most well-known trekking area of Nepal. Nevertheless, unlike popular trails like Annapurna and Everest, Langtang is a traffic-free trekking region. Langtang has numerous things making the trip here to be worthy. For instance, there’s a broad scope of mountains, and its ease of access is few perks of the Langtang valley trek.

Starting from Syabru Bensi at 1470 meters, the journey brings trekkers to beautiful Kyanjin Gompa at 3870 meters. Trekkers come across this height, bypassing several attractive ethnic villages and beguiling mountain sceneries. All through the journey, travelers come across Tamang and Sherpa ethnic towns surrounded by icy masses and peaks on the outskirt of Tibet. Similarly, the trekking path offers extraordinary various landscapes and cultures. The views of the mountains, specifically from Chorkari Ri, are simply breathtaking. Chorkari Ri is the vantage point at the height of 5050 in Kyanjin Gompa from where majestic views of Langtang Lirung (7246m), Kimshung (6781m), and Langshisha Ri (6370m).

Moreover, the trek is not all about mountains and their warm people. The walk encompasses meandering through dense forests of Langtang National Park. Langtang National Park covers the tropical lowland jungles to icy high altitude glaciers. The parks home arboreal red panda, Himalayan Tahr, Danphe, Himalayan monal, snow leopard, and many more. Thus, the region is a wonderland for bird watchers and animal lovers. Its vast jungle terrain and open pastureland are other stunning features of the Langtang valley trek.

Overall, traveling to this region will surely enjoy their time by indulging in the Himalayas and jungles’ serene beauty. And at the same time, experiencing the magnificent cultures and traditions of the Tamang and Sherpa community. Therefore, embark on the Langtang valley trek to take a glimpse of Nepal’s pastoral life before diving into a wilderness of sky-scraping mountains and crystalline lakes.
