EVEREST BASE CAMP

Mt. Everest is the highest mountain in the world, located in the mahalangur sub-range of the Himalayas which is between Nepal and Tibet. Nepal side the Everest located at Solu Khumbu district of Nepal. Itâ€™s also called the Everest region which is surrounded/ rotected by Everest National Park. The Everest region trekking is the most popular classical trekking in Nepal.it is also known as the roof of the world (8848.86m.), you can explore the Sherpa villages, sherpa culture, Buddhism as well as all those Buddhist creations such as Buddhist stupas, Mane (Monastries), prayer walls, prayer flags is one of the interesting things of this trek. Of course, you are seeing the rich & magnificent panoramic views of several different mountains in the Everest region including Mt.Everest, Nuptse, Lothse, Amadablam and many others. Everest region is the most traveller, adventure eekers bouquet list trek.

This classic walk through the Sherpa village of Solu Khumbu is a tough trek with a clear-cut goal to see Mount Everest. Trekking can be started after 10 hours drive by local bus/private vehicle to Jiir (17,00.00 m) or take the airstrip at Lukla (2,800 m), getting to the starting point of the trek itself, is an adventure. From Lukla you begin walking to the famous Sherpa village of Namche Bazaar and on to Tyangboche monastery with the mesmerizing Ama- Dablam as a backdrop. Take a trek over the glaciers to the foot of Everest, and you are in for the view of your lifetime. Although, this trek can be organized in several different itineraries, the Everest Base Camp and Kalapathar or the Gokyo pass are most popular among trekkers. Normally, the Everest Bese Camp trek is for 14 days, 16 days if you include the Kalapathar and will be for 18 days if you combine Gokyo Pass (Chola Pass) too. The Above itineraries include flying out of Kathmandu to Lukla and flying back from Lukla to Kathmandu. If you take the bus trip to Jiri and trek up to the above destination and fly to Kathmandu from Lukla, the trek will be 21,23 & 25 days accordingly. The other option is to drive to Jiri trek up then return to Jiri and drive by bus to Kathmandu for 28 to 35 days, then you have to trek back same way down from Lukla to Jiri but you can spend your pleasure time in mountain region rather than Kathmandu the busiest city. The highest pass of this trek is 5,546m.at Kalapather.
